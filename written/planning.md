## Design Intent

**Color Palette:**
My color palette reflects Seattle's Pacific Northwest atmosphere - deep ocean blues, misty grays, and evergreen accents. The primary color is a rich Seattle blue (#0066CC or similar deep blue) that captures the Puget Sound waters. Secondary colors include lighter sky blues, neutral grays for balance, and touches of forest green as accent. Three words that describe the feeling: **modern, refreshing, adventurous**.

**Typography:**
- **Headings:** I want a bold, clean sans-serif font that feels contemporary and urban - something that reflects Seattle's tech-forward, modern culture. (Consider fonts like Montserrat, Poppins, or Raleway)
- **Body text:** A readable, friendly sans-serif for body content that's easy to scan for tourists looking for information. (Consider fonts like Open Sans, Lato, or Roboto)

**Visual Choice Connecting to Seattle's Identity:**
Card-based layouts with subtle shadows to reflect the layered, elevated feel of Seattle's hilly terrain and modern architecture. Clean lines and generous white space that mirrors the minimalist, tech-influenced aesthetic Seattle is known for. Images should be prominent to capture the natural beauty and urban energy of the city.
